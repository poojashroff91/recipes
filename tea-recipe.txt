Ingredients:
 - 2 tablespoons sugar
 - 1 teaspoon tea powder
 - 1 1/2 cup water
 - 1/2 cup milk
 - 1 teaspoon cardamom powder
 
Directions:
 1. Bring water to a boil on a stove. 
 
 2. Add sugar, tea, cardamom powder and let the tea steep. 

 3. When the tea starts bubbling, add milk.
 
 3. Boil for a few minutes taking care not to let the milk boil over the vessel. 

 4. Strain and serve hot with biscuits.
